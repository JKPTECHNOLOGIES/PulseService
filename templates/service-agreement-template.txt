Prime Comfort Solutions
PLANNED SERVICE AGREEMENT

Prime Comfort Solutions, herein referred to as Prime, and Mr. Timothy Fox,
hereinafter referred to as Customer, hereby agrees that Prime will service the
air conditioning equipment at the following location: 8476 SE Mangrove Street,
Hobe Sound, FL 33458.

                          TYPE OF SERVICE
Planned Service Agreement
Planned Service on equipment with preferred rates on service repairs.

CONDITIONS

Routine Service consists of (4) regularly scheduled inspections with all labor
necessary to perform the inspections.

Inspections consist of the following: Inspect coils and/or clean as needed (in
place). Clean and/or replace standard air filters. Clean and treat condensate
drain pans & lines. Lubricate motors per OEM specifications. Measure temperature
difference through coil. Check all electrical connections and readings. Check
all electrical controls for proper operation. Check heating for seasonal
operation.

Normal business hours are 8:00 a.m. – 8:00 p.m. Monday through Saturday.
(Holidays excluded)

The equipment covered under this agreement must be in a condition satisfactory
to Prime. Pre-existing equipment deficiencies will be noted during the first
inspection. Customer may either authorize Prime to correct any deficiencies
noted at the quoted prices or cancel this agreement.

Equipment List
7 – Central air conditioning systems & 1 – Mini split system

The cost of this agreement is $2,100.00 per year, payable at time of acceptance.

The effective date of this agreement is 2/01/2026 – 1/31/2027. This agreement
will renew automatically annually and may be terminated by either party with 30
days written notice.

The air conditioning system or systems that are covered are intended to provide
a comfortable living environment for your home or business. While it is the
intent of our service agreement to curtail trouble calls, it is possible that
component failures or other system problems related to condensate or refrigerant
leaks may occur. As these circumstances are mechanical in nature and beyond our
immediate, direct control, Prime will accept no responsibility for any property
and or consequential damages resulting from any failures of this nature.
Environmental issues, such as mold and mildew, excessive dust or inconsistencies
in human comfort are also specifically excluded from the scope of your service
agreement.
